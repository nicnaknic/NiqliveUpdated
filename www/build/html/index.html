Activities/Interests
Photography
Photography has been a passion of mine for over five years, having first been introduced to it during an elective course back in highschool. Since then, I have had the opportunity to photograph many unique locations throughout Canada as well as various parts of Japan. Part of what I enjoy most about photography is the sense of adventure that comes with seeking out a unique place to photograph and the creative challenge of finding the perfect angle for a shot.
Computer Customization
Computing technology has been an interest of mine for as long as I can remember. Back in 2010 I started delving into modifying iOS devices by jailbreaking the OS so I could make little tweeks to the user experience. This soon led me to rooting my Android devices, installing custom ROMs and kernels, in order to personalize and tailor them to my specific needs. Eventually I got into computer hardware and have since built six custom computers both for personal use as well as for friends and family. I have always been known as the family ‘tech support,’ which offered me many opportunities to develop my interests and contributed largely to my decision to pursue a career in computer programming.
Indoor Gardening
Collecting, researching and caring for houseplants has become a major interest and time-sink of mine over the past couple years. What started off with a single lucky bamboo plant that I found on sale several years ago has quickly become a massive collection of tropical plants, each with their own unique aesthetic and care requirements. The diverse visual characteristics of these plants help give me creative inspiration when designing layouts or editing photos.
